NYC Free Museum Nights

Free Museums. Real Culture. Only in NYC.

About the Event

The NYC Museum Free Night Series is a recurring cultural event where museums across the city open their doors to the public at no cost. Guests can explore exhibits, attend live performances, and join interactive workshops in a lively, welcoming atmosphere.

The event is designed for students, tourists, families, and art lovers who want to experience New York's rich cultural scene without the barrier of admission fees. It's the perfect way to connect with art, history, and community in an accessible and exciting format.

Our guide to free museum nights, live performances, and more

What to Expect

  • Free Admission: No cost to enter participating museums
  • Special Events: Live performances, workshops, and artist talks
  • All Ages Welcome: Family-friendly programming throughout the evening
